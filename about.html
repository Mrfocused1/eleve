The Élevé Method®

Over 7 years of hands-on practice and teaching experience. Malika's approach integrates clinical precision with holistic body understanding — advanced face and body anatomy + physiology, lymphatic circulation, gut health and digestion, hormonal balance, inflammation, metabolic function and long-term wellbeing.

The Élevé Method® combines bespoke mapping, drainage sequences and tailored sculpting protocols for natural, visible results. Services span body contouring, lymphatic drainage, skin tightening, fat reduction, facial rejuvenation and post-operative support — all personalised to individual goals and needs.
